The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out her window for about an hour now.On her desk,lying between the copies of Nature ,New Scientist ,and all the other scientific journals her work had appeared in,was a well thumbed copy of On The Road .It had been Molly's fovourite book since college ,and the longer she spent in these four walls the morse she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in this room,sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde qoute proclaiming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do.Although many considered her pioneering work,unravelling the secrets of the IIama DNA,to be an achievement,Molly did think she had sommething better to do.
